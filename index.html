About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Civil Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), supported by the prestigious Chinese Scholarship Council (CSC) Scholarship.

My research focuses on the phase transition mechanisms of clustered shallow flow-slide disaster chains under rainfall and freeze-thaw cycles, with applications in early warning systems for mountainous regions of southeastern China.

I integrate SPH numerical modeling, GIS-based analysis, and physical centrifuge testing to uncover failure mechanisms in complex landslide systems.

Beyond research, I mentor students and am passionate about computational mechanics and data-driven geotechnical analysis.

My work has been published in leading international journals including Transportation Infrastructure Geotechnology, Engineering Geology, and Journal of Road Engineering, reflecting a commitment to rigorous, peer-reviewed science.

I am currently involved in a National Key Research Project on dynamic landslide chain processes in China's southeastern mountains, contributing to the development of robust early warning systems that can protect lives and infrastructure in hazard-prone regions.

Research Projects

🏔️

Phase Transition of Clustered Shallow Flow-Slide Disaster Chains in Northeastern China

National Key Project examining dynamic processes and hazard assessment of complex chains of rainfall-induced landslides and debris flows in southeastern China. Tools: LPF3D, Abaqus, GIS.

Current PhD · National Key Project
❄️

Slope Stability Analysis under Freeze and Thaw Cycles with Joint Frame Anchors and Anti-Slip Piles Stabilization

Case study of the Xinyi Village landslide in Northeast China. Used FEM via Abaqus with shear strength reduction analysis to reveal failure mechanisms under repeated freeze-thaw cycles.

Master's Project
🛣️

Characterization and Subgrade Strength Prediction of Expansive Soils in Pakistan's Potohar Region with Stone Dust Stabilization

Led a team of 6 to develop correlation models for CBR prediction from UCC values for indigenous subgrade soils in Pakistan's Potohar region. Published at CSCE 2021.
